Umbrella?

The easiest way to get your weather


The Site

Umbrella? is a simple weather website powered by the OpenWeatherMap and MapQuest APIs for weather and location information, respectively. It provides clear answers to the question "Do I need an umbrella?" No complex maps or graphs. Its clean UI and easy layout make it perfect for quick answers.

The Purpose

This website is a show of my front-end developement skills. Although it is not that complex, it still shows my mastery of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It shows I can utilize external libraries (Bootsrap) and APIS (OpenWeatherMap and MapQuest) for design and function. This is my first completely solo project, done with no tutorial or external help, and I only expect to grow from here on out.
